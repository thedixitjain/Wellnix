About Wellnix

We're building AI-powered tools to make personalized health guidance accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Health decisions shouldn't require a personal nutritionist or trainer. We believe AI can democratize access to expert-level health guidance — from understanding what's in your food to perfecting your exercise form.

The Story

Wellnix started from a simple frustration: nutrition labels are confusing, and gym form advice is expensive or inaccessible.

We asked: What if your phone could instantly tell you whether a food is right for you? What if it could be your personal trainer, giving real-time feedback on your squat form?

That question became Wellnix — an AI-powered health companion that puts personalized nutrition and fitness guidance in everyone's pocket.
